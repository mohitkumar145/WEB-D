k bar ek chhota sa gaon tha, jahan ek Ramoo naam ka ladka rehta tha. Ramoo bahut hi hardworking aur honest tha, lekin uski family thodi poor thi. Ek din, Ramoo forest mein wood cut karne gaya. Garmi bahut zyada thi, toh wo ek river ke paas rest karne baitha. Usne apni axe (kulhaadi) side mein rakhi, par galti se wo slip hokar gahre paani mein gir gayi. Ramoo bahut upset ho gaya kyunki uske paas bas wahi ek tool tha. Tabhi river se ek water fairy bahar aayi. Usne pucha, "Beta, tum kyun ro rahe ho?" Ramoo ne bataya, "Meri axe paani mein gir gayi hai, ab main kaam kaise karunga?" Fairy ne smile kiya aur paani mein dive lagayi. Wo ek Golden Axe lekar bahar aayi aur pucha, "Kya ye tumhari hai?" Ramoo bola, "Nahi, ye meri nahi hai." Fairy ne phir dive kiya aur is baar Silver Axe lekar aayi. Ramoo ne phir mana kar diya. "Nahi, ye bhi meri nahi hai." Final time, fairy ne uski purani Iron Axe nikali. Ramoo khushi se chilla utha, "Haan! Yahi meri axe hai! Thank you so much!" Fairy uski honesty dekh kar bahut impress hui aur usne Ramoo ko reward ke taur par teeno axes de di. Ramoo ne seekha ki Honesty is the best policy.
